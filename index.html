--- title: "Austin H Patton" layout: single author_profile: true excerpt: "What is life?" header: overlay_image: austinhpatton.github.io/Images/HomeHeader.jpg --- # Research ### Bridging the gap between micro- and macro-evolution Broadly speaking, my research is focused on the processes of hybridization, adaptation, and diversification. To study these, I leverage both comparative phylogenetic methods at the macroevolutionary scale, and population/landscape genomic approaches at the microevolutionary scale.
